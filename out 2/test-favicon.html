Troubleshooting

If you don't see the favicon in your browser tab, try these steps:

  1. Hard refresh the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
  2. Clear your browser cache
  3. Try a different browser
  4. Check if the favicon files are accessible by clicking the links above
